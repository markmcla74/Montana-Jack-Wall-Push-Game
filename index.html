Montana Jack Wall Push Game

Game Rules

Player Controls

  • Player 1: A = Rest, S = Push, D = Super Push
  • Player 2: J = Rest, K = Push, L = Super Push

Energy System

  • Start: 3 energy units
  • Rest: +4 energy
  • Push: –1 energy, moves wall 1 unit
  • Super Push: –3 energy, moves wall 2 units
